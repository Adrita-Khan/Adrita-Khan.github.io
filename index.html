Featured Research

Testing Modified Gravity Theories

My research focuses on testing modified gravity theories, specifically f(R) gravity and Horndeski theories, by analyzing cross-correlated power spectra. The primary objective is to examine the Galaxy-Galaxy power spectrum, Galaxy-CMB lensing cross-power spectrum, and CMB lensing auto-power spectrum to detect signatures of modified gravity that deviate from the standard ΛCDM model.
